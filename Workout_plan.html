How to make it work

The plan is only as good as how you run it.

⏱️
Rest times
60–90 sec for isolations (cables, curls, laterals). 2–3 min for compounds (hip thrust, RDL, overhead press).
📈
Progressive overload
Add reps before adding weight. Once you hit the top of the rep range comfortably, add 2–2.5 kg next session.
🕐
Session length
Each session runs 55–65 minutes at this volume. Warm up 5 min, keep rest disciplined.
🌙
Recovery
Thursday is full rest. Sleep 7–9 hrs — that's when the shape actually changes. Protein 1.6–2 g per kg bodyweight.
🔄
Alternate schedule option
If recovery feels hard, switch to 3 on / 1 off / 3 on instead of 6 consecutive days.
🌅
Morning habit
Do 3 sets of stomach vacuums fasted every morning — takes 3 minutes and adds up fast over weeks.
